About My Game List

@smolcronch

Hi there! My name is Jessica and I created My Game List to make it easier for gamers to keep track of their video game collection and connect with other gamers. I love video games and have been playing them since I was 5 years old.


MGL aims to make it easier to manage all your games and lets you make notes and upload images of your gameplay. We use IGDB API to get all the up-to-date information on all the video games out there.


Enjoy your adventure, gamers!
